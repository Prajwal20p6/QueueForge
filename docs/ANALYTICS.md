# Business Intelligence & Analytics Guide

Overview of the QueueForge Business Intelligence layer.

---

## 📈 Components
1.  **Business Metrics**: Compiles total processed, success rates, and cost estimates.
2.  **Insight Generation**: Scans historical average latencies to alert trends.
3.  **Scheduled Reporting**: Compiles digests to send to stakeholders.
4.  **Adhoc Querying**: Sandbox allowing safe read-only SQL diagnostics.
