# Product Vision

## Vision Statement
To build a highly resilient, low-latency, and audit-logged asynchronous event delivery engine that guarantees zero message loss and exactly-once delivery semantics for enterprise integrations.

## Core Pillars
1.  **Guaranteed Reliability**: Never drop a single task result, even under complete network partitions.
2.  **Downstream Protection**: Integrate circuit breakers and bulkheads to prevent cascading resource exhaustion on third-party integrations.
3.  **End-to-End Observability**: Maintain detailed audit trails and distributed trace lineages for every single task ingestion.
