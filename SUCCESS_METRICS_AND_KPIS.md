# Success Metrics and KPIs

Target indicators evaluating platform health.

---

## 🏗️ Targets
*   **Availability SLA**: Target `99.9%` monthly uptime.
*   **Success Ratio**: Target `> 99.95%` deliveries rate.
*   **Mean Time to Recovery (MTTR)**: Target `< 15 minutes` recovery windows.
*   **Ingestion Latency**: Target `P95 < 5s`.
